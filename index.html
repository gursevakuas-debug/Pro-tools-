About One Page Tools Pro

One Page Tools Pro is your comprehensive solution for all PDF-related tasks. We offer 27 free online tools that empower you to manage, edit, and convert PDF files directly in your browser without the need for software installation or file uploads to external servers.

Why PDF Tools Are Essential

PDF (Portable Document Format) has become the standard for document sharing and distribution. Whether you're a student, professional, or business owner, you'll likely encounter PDF files regularly. Our tools help you:

  • Combine multiple documents into a single PDF
  • Extract specific pages from large PDF files
  • Reduce file size for easier sharing
  • Convert between PDF and other formats
  • Add annotations, signatures, and watermarks
  • Protect sensitive information

Core Tools Overview

Merge PDF: Combine multiple PDF files into a single document with our intuitive merging tool.

Split PDF: Extract specific pages or split a PDF into multiple files based on your needs.

Compress PDF: Reduce file size without significant quality loss for easier sharing and storage.

Conversions: Convert PDFs to Word, PowerPoint, Excel, images, and vice versa.

Editing: Add text, images, shapes, and annotations directly to your PDF files.

Security: Add passwords, watermarks, and digital signatures to protect your documents.

All processing happens directly in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security for your documents. We never upload your files to our servers, so you can work with sensitive documents with complete peace of mind.
